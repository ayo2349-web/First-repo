About Us

Your Trusted Aluminum Roofing Partner

GTS Aluminum & Roofing System has been providing premium roofing solutions for over 25 years. We specialize in aluminum roofing installations, repairs, and maintenance for both residential and commercial properties across Nigeria.

Our commitment to quality, durability, and customer satisfaction has made us a leader in the roofing industry. We use only the finest materials and employ skilled craftsmen to ensure every project exceeds expectations.

Client Satisfaction

Founded in Akure, Ondo State, GTS has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted roofing companies. From humble beginnings, we have expanded our operations to cover residential estates, commercial buildings, and industrial facilities nationwide.

Every member of our team is trained to the highest standards, ensuring that each installation meets our strict quality benchmarks. We stand behind our work with an industry-leading 50-year warranty.
